House Rules
💵
The Door Is Yours
You run your own door and keep 100% of what you make. The $100 venue fee is the only thing the house takes. Bring your own change — ones and fives if you're charging at the door. We may not have it available by showtime.
🎶
Originals Only
No cover bands. No tribute acts. This room is for original music only. Minimum four acts on the bill unless you have more than six — in which case, talk to us. The show runs until at least 1am.
📣
You Promote It
We'll post your flyers and blast the show day-of. But the promotion is on you. Get on social media, share relentlessly, submit to Houston Press (it's free). You keep what you make at the door — so make it count.
📋
Deposits & Cancellations
Out-of-town or first-time bookers pay a $100 deposit via Stripe to lock the date. If you cancel, the deposit is gone. Sound engineer is on-site — tipping is strongly encouraged. Reach out early if plans change.
Common Questions
How far out do I need to book? +
Call anytime after 11am at 713-923-2837 and we'll see what dates are open. The earlier the better — especially for Friday and Saturday nights. Last-minute requests are handled case by case.
Do I need to pay the $100 up front? +
If you're a first-time booker or coming from out of town, a $100 Stripe deposit is required to hold the date. If you have questions before paying, contact us through Instagram @LogosMinds.
Can I set up ticketing for my show? +
Yes. Through Underground Showtime you can have a full ticketing page live within 24 hours of booking confirmation — custom tiers, Stripe checkout, QR door check-in, and a full revenue report after. Flat fee, no percentage cuts. Totally optional, but it's there.
What's included with the room? +
Full PA system (5,000+ watts), 200 sq ft drum riser, stage lighting (8 cans / 10 effects / 2 strobes), full stage monitors, and a sound engineer on-site. Bring your own change, stamps, wristbands, and flyers.
Can I use the space for a film or music video shoot? +
Yes — film and music video shoots are welcome. Call in advance at 713-923-2837 to discuss details, scheduling, and any specific setup you need for production.
I'm a band looking to jump on an existing show — can you help? +
Call the venue and they'll see if there's an existing show that fits your genre and get you connected with that promoter. They don't set up shows on your behalf, but they can make the connection if the fit is right.
