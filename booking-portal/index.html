Quick FAQ

What are you doing with Venue Portal right now?
Venue Portal is being built into a simple booking flow for underground rooms, with White Swan Live up first and more rooms getting added as things settle after the move.
How does booking work?
Promoters lock a date with a flat $100 venue fee, keep 100% of the door, and then finalize lineup and show details after the request comes through.
Is this only for White Swan?
No. White Swan Live is the current live example, and the plan is to keep adding more Houston rooms into the same portal as they come online.
