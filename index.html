House Rules
🕐
Load-In & Access
Load-in begins at the time confirmed in your booking. Access is not granted before your window. All equipment must be out of the space within 1 hour of show end. Repeated late exits affect future booking eligibility.
👥
Capacity
You are responsible for maintaining attendance within your confirmed capacity. Overselling is grounds for immediate termination of the event. Door count is tracked in real time through our check-in system.
📋
Documentation
All bookings require organizer ID, event description, and estimated headcount at time of confirmation. Photo documentation of the space at load-in and load-out is required for all events.
💳
Payment & Cancellation
Booking fee is due upon confirmation. Cancellations made less than 72 hours before the event are non-refundable. Rescheduling is available with 5 or more days notice subject to availability.
Common Questions
How far in advance do I need to book? +
We recommend submitting your request at least 2 weeks out. We can accommodate shorter timelines but availability isn't guaranteed. For large-scale or multi-artist events, 3–4 weeks gives us proper runway.
Does the venue come with ticketing? +
Not automatically, but it's available from the moment your date is confirmed. We work with Underground Showtime for all ticketing ops — flat fee, Stripe checkout, QR door management, and full post-show data. You can add it to any booking.
What's included in the booking fee? +
The booking fee covers venue access for your confirmed time block, in-house audio (where applicable), and coordination support through your event. Ticketing ops, production, and staffing are separate and quoted on request.
Can I bring outside production or sound? +
Yes. You're welcome to bring your own production, lighting, and sound for spaces that don't include in-house systems. All outside equipment must be confirmed in advance so we can accommodate load-in timing and space setup.
What happens if my show gets cancelled? +
Cancellations with 5 or more days notice are eligible for a full reschedule. Cancellations under 72 hours are non-refundable but can be credited toward a future booking at our discretion. Reach out as early as possible — we work with you where we can.
