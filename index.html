BOOKING A SHOW AT THE SWAN LIVE

1. $100 Venue Fee

If this is new to you, it’s been at least a year since you’ve booked a show. Out-of-town promoters or first-timers must pay the $100 upfront via PayPal. If you cancel, you lose the fee/deposit.

2. Tipping the Sound Engineer

Encouraged.

3. You Run Your Own Door

You keep 100% of what you make at the door.

4. Bring These Items

• Plenty of change (ones and fives)
• Marker, stamp, or wristbands

5. Minimum 4 Bands

Shows start no earlier than 9pm on Fri/Sat and should run until at least 1am.

6. PROMOTE YOUR SHOW

Bring flyers. Submit to Houston Press. Share relentlessly on social media. We will also promote it, but the majority is on you.

7. We Don’t Set Up Shows

Out-of-town bands: Call and we’ll try to connect you with a promoter. We won’t build a full bill for you.

8. Movie & Music Video Shoots Welcome

Call in advance for details.
